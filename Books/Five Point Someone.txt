Five Point Someone is Chetan Bhagat’s debut novel that brings out a dark and amusing perspective on the Indian higher education system. The story is set in the premises of an IIT, with three students from different backgrounds as the protagonists. The three guys become close friends while they try hard to keep their place in the exceedingly respected institute. The story is mostly narrated in first person by Hari. Hari, Alok, and Ryan join IIT with a determination to come out successfully as the best graduates. However, their course of life becomes utter chaos when their grades fall lower than they had ever expected. The book subtly talks about the mundane education system even in the country’s foremost engineering institutes.
