Robert Langdon, a renowned Harvard symbologist is the protagonist of Angels And Demons, a prequel to The Da Vinci Code. A phone call from the Director of the CERN summons him to a Swiss facility where a strange symbol is found seared onto the chest of a dead physicist. When Langdon examines the body, he discovers that it is a symbol of the Illuminati, an ancient but powerful underground brotherhood. Langdon fears that this brotherhood may have returned to seek out their vendetta against the Catholic Church.Langdon’s suspicions are confirmed when the Illuminati’s messenger announces that they have hidden a time bomb in the heart of the Vatican City. Langdon teams up with Vittoria Vetra, the dead physicist’s daughter in order to locate the time bomb.
